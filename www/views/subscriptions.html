For Individual Developers

If you, as an individual, are purchasing PIO Plus Subscription using your own funds, then the PIO Plus Personal Subscription plans are right for you.

Personal PIO Plus Subscriptions cannot be purchased by companies in any way or form, nor can a company reimburse an individual for a subscription he/she purchased. However, an individual may use Personal PIO Plus Subscription that he/she privately purchased on any hardware, including company hardware.

If you are not sure that PIO Plus Personal Subscription is right for you, check the subscription comparison matrix.

For complete details, please check our standard PIO Plus License Agreement.

For Business and Organizations

Legal entities, including companies and organizations (both for-profit and non-profit), requiring the software for general commercial use qualify for the PIO Plus Business Subscription plans. A legal entity may use the software under PIO Plus Business Subscription on any computer, operating system, and by any developer within a legal entity, provided that the total number of concurrent users never exceeds the number of subscriptions purchased by that legal entity.

If you are not sure that Commercial PIO Plus Subscription is right for you, check the subscription comparison matrix.

For complete details, please check our standard PIO Plus License Agreement.

For Non-commercial Use

Private individuals (including students and teachers), requiring the software for non-commercial purposes qualify for the PIO Plus Non-commercial Subscription plans.

PIO Plus Non-commercial Subscriptions cannot be purchased by schools/universities in any way or form, nor can a school/university reimburse a student/teacher for a subscription he/she purchased. However, a student/teacher may use Student/Teacher PIO Plus Subscription that he/she privately purchased on any hardware, including school/university hardware.

For complete details, please check our standard PIO Plus License Agreement.
